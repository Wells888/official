What is colour temperature?

Hopolite's LED Lighting offer led strip lights in warm, natural, pure and cool white with colour temperatures measured in Kelvin (K). The lower the colour temperature is,the warmer the light given by the LED:
Warm white = 2700K-3200K
Natural white = 4000K-4500K
Pure white = 6000K-6500K
Cool white > 7000K

What is CRI?

CRI is a figure which feedback the real objects colors. Higher CRI with higher cost. For some LED lights, the CRI is higher than 80. But for the most common LED lights, the CRI higher than 70 is OK at most of applications.
